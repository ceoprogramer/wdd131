Site Purpose:

This project focuses on the development of a website dedicated to showcasing the beautiful tourist attractions of Yucatan's natural areas, Mexico. The main goal is to create a visually appealing and information-rich digital platform that inspires visitors to explore the beaches, cenotes, and archaeological sites, thereby boosting local and regional tourism.

This website will provide info about:

  • Beaches
  • Natural cenotes
  • Arqueological sites
  • A contact us page

Scenarios

1. A tourist looking for info about Yucatan

What touristic places can I visit in Yucatan?

2. A foreigner who came to live in Yucatan

Where can I find information about natural destinations in Yucatan?
